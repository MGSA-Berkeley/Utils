Welcome to the office draw website!

Use the up and down arrow keys or the number keys 7, 8, 9, 1, 0 to navigate to different floors of Evans. You can also click on the floor number icons on the right.

Click on an office to see who's already in it.

Click on someone's name on the right to see where their office is on the map.

Looking for a different year's information? Click these: 2025, 2024, 2023, 2022, 2021, 2020, 2019, 2018
